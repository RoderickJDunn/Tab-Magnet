Selecting Results

  • Use your mouse or ↑↓ arrow keys to select results
  • Tab Results
    • Click or press ENTER to jump to selected tab
    • Click the 'X' on a tab result to close that tab
  • History Results
    • Click or press ENTER to open the selected history item in a new tab
    • See Shortcut Keys for other ways to open history results
